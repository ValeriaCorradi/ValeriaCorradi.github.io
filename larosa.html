BOMBONIERE LA ROSA ECOMMERCE

overview

For this project, I had the opportunity to redesign the user interface for Bomboniere La Rosa, a thriving e-commerce platform specializing in party favours.

The project focused on developing a modern, user-friendly e-commerce experience that seamlessly guides users through the enjoyable process of selecting and purchasing their favourite products.

MY ROLE

As the UI designer on this project, my primary responsibilities included:

  • Designing the visual layout and interface elements

  • Ensuring a cohesive and professional look aligned with the brand identity

  • Optimizing user experience and navigation

  • Collaborating with another designer and incorporating their insights

Challenges and Solutions

Balancing Elegance and Warmth

A key challenge was developing a design that showcased the company's elegance and professionalism while inspiring warmth and familiarity to potential clients.
To tackle this, I:

  • Employed a sleek, contemporary design featuring a relevant color scheme

  • Integrated warm, relatable visuals and icons

  • Crafted distinct call-to-action buttons to promote user interaction

Drive Conversion

Given the nature of e-commerces, it was essential to convey details in a straightforward manner.
I approached this by:

  • Developing user-friendly navigation options

  • Crafting engaging visuals and infographics to showcase ideas and products

  • Establishing a clear information hierarchy using typography and layout

Responsive Design

Ensuring the website looked great and functioned well across all devices was another challenge.
I addressed this by:

  • Implementing a mobile-first design approach

  • Creating flexible layouts that adapt to different screen sizes

  • Optimizing images and elements for faster loading on mobile devices

HIGHLIGHTS AND ACHIEVEMENTS

Working on this project was incredibly rewarding.
Some highlights include:

  • Collaborating with another designer to brainstorm ideas and exchange knowledge

  • Seeing the positive impact of the new design on user engagement

  • Receiving positive feedback from the client on the improved user experience

  • Successfully translating the company's vision of familiarity, joyfulness, quality, originality into a visual design

Outcome

The redesigned website now effectively showcases La Rosa's products and craftsmanship in party favours.
It aligns perfectly with their vision:

"Our goal is to establish ourselves as the go-to for anyone wishing to celebrate their special moments with certified, original, and high-quality favours.
La Rosa is committed to providing a unique and memorable experience, guiding customers with passion and expertise in selecting the ideal keepsake, capable of evoking lasting emotions over time."
